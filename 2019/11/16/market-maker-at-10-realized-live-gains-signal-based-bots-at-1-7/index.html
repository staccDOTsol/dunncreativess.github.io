What’s Next

Coindex and myself and some of the bot ideationmasters we’ve mentioned will be working on ways to hedge losses in the case our bots see stoplosses.

What this means for Coindex at least is to use different entries for long calls+puts on the same instrument and expiry, which is called a long straddle or strangle. If the price moves up OR down significantly (causing losses on market makers) then the options will go out of money and simultaneously in the money at the same time, and at leverage on a deriviative one of them will be worth some serious money!
